Tesla Employee Policy 2026
Section 1: Work Hours All employees must be at their desks by 9:00 AM. Remote work is only allowed on Fridays for the Engineering team. Marketing must be in-office every day.
Section 2: Travel Expenses Employees will be reimbursed for travel. However, the limit for meals is $50 per day for Junior Staff. For Senior Management, the meal limit is $150 per day. All receipts must be submitted via the internal portal.


Section 3: Security Security is our top priority. Never share your badge. If you lose your badge, the replacement fee is $25. This fee is waived for the first loss.

The emergency passcode for the main gate is 99283.

Tesla employees must wear blue badges. These badges allow access to the cafeteria.
